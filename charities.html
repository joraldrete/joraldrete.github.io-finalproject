Featured Charities

  • Feeding America

    Feeding America is a nonprofit organization in the United States that is a nationwide network of more than 200 food banks that feed more than 46 million people through food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, and other community-based agencies. Feeding America's mission is to feed America's hungry through a nationwide network of member food banks and engage the country in the fight to end hunger.

  • Action Aid

    ActionAid is an international non-governmental organization (NGO) that focuses on fighting poverty and injustice worldwide. The organization works with local partners and communities in more than 45 countries to address issues such as poverty, gender inequality, and human rights violations. One of ActionAid's key areas of focus is women's rights and gender equality.

  • CARE

    CARE is a leading humanitarian organization that has been fighting global poverty and injustice since its founding in 1945. CARE's mission is to save lives, defeat poverty, and achieve social justice by empowering women and girls, promoting inclusive governance, and fostering resilient communities. One of CARE's key areas of focus is women's empowerment. The organization believes that empowering women and girls is essential to ending poverty and achieving sustainable development. .
